What is Windsurf?

Windsurf is Codeium's AI-powered code editor, designed as a direct competitor to Cursor. It combines traditional IDE features with advanced AI capabilities, including flow-aware completions and AI-powered debugging.


Built by the team behind Codeium (a fast-growing autocomplete tool), Windsurf brings their AI expertise to a full-featured editor.

Key Features

  • ▸ Flow Completions — AI understands entire coding flows, not just lines
  • ▸ Multi-File Editing — Edit across multiple files intelligently
  • ▸ Terminal AI — Get AI help in your terminal
  • ▸ Debug Assistant — AI-powered debugging and error explanation
